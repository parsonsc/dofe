There's a Challenger in all of us

That language you keep saying you'll learn. The mountain you've always wanted to climb. The chocolate addiction you've always wanted to kick. 2016 is your year. As part of the DofE's Diamond Anniversary celebrations, we're inviting you to take the DofE Diamond Challenge; a challenge of your choice, to raise money and help vulnerable young people to shine.

Find yours for them

You don't have to decide on your challenge immediately. Sign up now and we'll send your exclusive Challenge Companion. A journal and keepsake to help you at each stage of your challenge; from choosing, to planning, taking part and remembering it forever.

All we ask is that you raise a minimum of £60 (it is our 60th birthday, after all). Then give it your all, complete your challenge and you won't just be rewarded with a limited edition Diamond pin and an incredible sense of achievement. You'll help to transform the lives of young people.

It's our Diamond Anniversary

The Duke of Edinburgh's Award is a charity that has been dedicated to helping young people give it their all since 1956. With every challenge they take on, and every DofE Award they work towards, young people from all walks of life have been given a chance to shine.

Shine for them

The DofE helps open doors and transform the lives of young people like Emma, who was struggling to find her way in life. But as a charity, we're nothing without the support of people like you. Take the DofE Diamond Challenge, raise money and you'll give even more vulnerable young people like Emma that stepping stone they need towards a brighter future.
